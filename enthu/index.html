ABOUT US

When we say Enthusia is one of the biggest sports festivals in India, It's no exaggeration! Graced with some of the best sports facilities one could ask for, this annual festival is sure to set your heartbeat racing, and give you that adrenaline rush once again. Some of the most skilled and talented sportsmen will lock horns in front of hoards of fans spurring the players on to bring the best out of them. V.J.T.I. can boast of having a cricket ground, a football ground, basketball court, tennis clay courts (one of the few in India!), carrom and table tennis facilities, a badminton court, and lots more. Enthusia provides ample opportunities to develop your creativity, leadership qualities, team building and managerial skills. Our vision is to nurture sports talent and make realize their full potential.
